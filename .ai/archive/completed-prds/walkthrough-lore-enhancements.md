## Lore Sources

### Source 1: POE2 Wiki (https://www.poe2wiki.net/wiki/Lore)
- **Act 1:** Primordial forests of Ogham, corruption and disease, player escaped execution by Count Geonor, evaded The Executioner by jumping into ocean, land marked by centuries of conflict between Ezomyte and Eternal peoples
- **Act 2:** Vastiri region - once lush and verdant, now crimson sands, blackened wastelands, harsh saltflats, nomadic caravans traverse the terrain
- **Act 3:** Inner continent's jungles, decaying ziggurats of ancient Vaal civilization (once pinnacle of power), Oswald the treasure hunter explored Utzaal

### Source 2: Maxroll Campaign Overview (https://maxroll.gg/poe2/resources/path-of-exile-2-campaign-overview)

**Act 1 - Phaaryl Region:**
- Track the "Seed of Corruption" stolen by Geonor
- Work with the mysterious Hooded One
- Battlefields still populated by undead from ancient war
- Dark forests, sepulchers, blighted landscapes
- Ruined town of Ogham
- Count's Manor - final battle in lowest level
- Iron Count boss: "fights first with confidence, then with wild desperation, eventually transforming into a twisted wolf"
- Countess Oriana escapes with the Seed after Geonor's defeat, heading toward Vastiri Plains

**Act 2 - Vastiri Desert:**
- Ardura Caravan as base
- Allied with Ardura faction against Faridun rivals
- Land of Seven Waters
- Reclaim Seed of Corruption from Jamanra
- Key zones: Traitor's Passage, Valley of Titans, city of Keth, Spires of Deshar, Dreadnought (final area)
- Djinn Balbala in Traitor's Passage for Trial of Sekhemas

**Act 3 - Utzaal Jungle:**
- Ancient Vaal ruins in Aggorat
- Aggorat was crown jewel of Atziri's empire
- Soul Cores power Vaal technology
- Doryani at pyramid's Apex in his Machinarium

**Act 4 - Ngamakanui:**
- Port city of Kingsmarch
- Multiple islands accessible by Kalguur captain
- Choose order to explore self-contained island stories
- Goblins tame wildlife and worship local giants on some islands
